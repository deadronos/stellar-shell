# Stellar Shell: Gameplay Design Document

## Core Concept

**Loop**: `Mine Asteroids` -> `Acquire Matter` -> `Expand Drone Swarm` -> `Build Dyson Sphere` -> `Generate Energy` -> `Prestige/Upgrade`

## 1. Voxel Mining & Resources

We leverage the fully voxelized nature of asteroids.

- **Manual Mining**: Player uses a laser tool to break individual voxel blocks.
- **Drone Mining**: Drones swarm asteroids, "eating" them voxel by voxel.
    - *Visuals*: Blocks shrink or particle effects show transfer to drone.
- **Resources**:
    - **Matter** (Raw): From stone/surface. Used for drone construction and basic frames.
    - **Rare Matter / Rare Elements** (Advanced): Represented in runtime as `rareMatter`. Found in seeded **Rare Ore** veins generated by per-system noise variation. Used for advanced sphere components (panels → shell upgrades) and certain tech upgrades (e.g. laser, auto‑replicator).

## 2. The Dyson Sphere (The Megaproject)

The ultimate goal is to enclose the central star at `0,0,0`.
Construction is **phased** and **voxel-based**.

### Phase 0: The Skeleton

- **Mechanism**: Player places "Frame Blueprints". The runtime seeds an initial spherical Dyson blueprint skeleton, then optional Auto-Blueprint expansion adds deterministic follow-up blueprints ordered by 3-D distance from the star, covering the full sphere volume from the inside out.
- **Cost**: Blueprints themselves are free, but drones consume matter to build each frame when they arrive; the overall matter cost is therefore high.
- **Gameplay**: Drones must physically fly to the construction site to "weld" the frames.
- **Incremental Hook**: Unlocking "Auto-Blueprint" allows blueprint generation to continue automatically. New nodes are placed in deterministic sphere-aware order (closest to the star first), ensuring expansion looks and behaves like an ever-growing sphere rather than a planar disk.

### Phase 1: Solar Panels

- **Mechanism**: Fill frames with panels.
- **Output**: Generates **Energy**.
- **Visual**: Hexagonal or geodesic dome structure enclosing the star.

### Phase 2: The Shell

- A solid shell for maximum energy capture. Requires rare matter sourced from asteroid Rare Ore veins.

## 3. Idle / Incremental Mechanics

### Currency & Resources

- **Matter**: Primary currency. High volume; surface blocks give 1 matter each while asteroid core blocks give 2 matter when mined. Recycling a frame also refunds its matter cost.
- **Energy**: Generated by Dyson sphere parts. Used for upgrades.
- **Research Data**: Generated by drones assigned to the **Explorer** role while they are in the `EXPLORING` loop. The `ADVANCED_EXPLORER` upgrade doubles research from those assigned explorers.
- **Swarm Roles**: The HUD exposes `+` / `-` controls for **Miner**, **Builder**, and **Explorer** role targets. Any drones left unassigned by the player are auto-distributed as evenly as possible across the three roles, with leftover priority `Miner -> Builder -> Explorer`.

### Upgrades (The Tech Tree)

- **Fast Drill** ("Swarm Intelligence" in earlier docs): Drones mine 50 % faster and yield more resources from asteroids.
- **Thrusters**: Drones move faster; critical for large distances between asteroids and the sphere.
- **Laser Efficiency**: Player laser yields 2× resources per block (despite the name, it doesn’t change tick count).  Also applies when recycling construction components.
- **Auto-Replicator**: Drones automatically build new drones if resources allow; this is toggleable at runtime once the upgrade is owned.

### Prestige: "System Jump"

- Current implementation exposes **System Jump** when Dyson progression is prestige-ready and energy generation exceeds the runtime threshold (`energyGenerationRate > 100`).
- **Action**: Move the entire sphere/swarm to a new, richer star system.
- **Reset**: Matter, rare matter, energy, drones, and per-system upgrades.
- **Keep**: Prestige progression, Stellar Crystals (prestige currency), research, and progression to the next seeded system.
- **New System**: Denser asteroid variation, fresh seeded topology, and room for additional exotic-material mechanics.

## 4. Implementation Plan (MVP)

1. **Central Star**: Add a visual star at world origin.
2. **Sphere Blueprint**: Calculate positions for a geodesic sphere and render them as ghost voxels.
3. **Construction Logic**: Update `BrainSystem` to prioritize sphere frames when blueprints exist.
4. **UI**: Add an upgrades panel and swarm role allocator.

## 5. Technical Considerations

- **Performance**: A full sphere is too many voxels.
    - *Solution*: Use megastructure objects (instanced meshes) for completed sections, only using voxels for the active construction frontier.
- **Orbits**: Asteroids can optionally orbit the star slowly, with runtime controls exposed in Settings.
